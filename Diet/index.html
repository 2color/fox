Diet Information

What do foxes eat?
Foxes are omnivorous hunters, and will generally eat anything easily available and easy enough to catch. This includes small mammals, birds, as well as large insects. They consume meat mainly in wintertime, whereas in spring, summer and autumn they primarily consume fruit, berries and nuts.
